qa40x-rs Audio Analyzer — installation notes
============================================

1. Drag "qa40x-rs Audio Analyzer.app" onto the Applications folder
   (both are in this window).

2. Plug in your QA402 / QA403 analyzer over USB and launch the app.
   No driver is needed on macOS.

This is an open-source project — you can audit or rebuild it yourself:
https://github.com/GarageDeveloper/qa40x-rs

Trouble or questions:
https://github.com/GarageDeveloper/qa40x-rs/issues
